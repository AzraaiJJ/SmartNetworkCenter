About Us

Vision

To make everyday life better with the Internet and creating a better digital learning environment.

Mission

To solve the Internet problem and enhance user satisfaction while using the Internet.

Objectives

  1. To find the causes poor Wifi connection.
  2. To collect the data from students about the Wifi problem.
  3. To help the students report about the Wifi issues.

Why?

This website is specially designed for students that having a Wifi problem at the college. Besides , poor Wifi connecting can affect our learning experience, especially during online classes . This website aim to helps student understand the possible causes of Wifi issues and provide them with easier way to report these problems through our website.
